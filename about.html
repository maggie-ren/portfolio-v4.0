About me

Peilin(Maggie) here, I am a UI/UX designer based in Raleigh, NC. My background is in architecture and architectural design which I got a bachelor in China, Masters in UK plus a couple years of industry experiences. From those years I realized that my strong passion for design will not be used to its full potential in this industry due to limitations(eg: Project budget, Structure and building materials).

After leaving the industry I freelance as a graphic designer, during that time I was introduced to UI/UX designers. From there I was hooked and pulled into the world of UI/UX. I immediately signed up for one of the best programs available. Now I am a UX/UI designer who enjoys crafting a good story and creating user-centric, delightful experiences.
